## Ingredients
* 1 pound ground turkey
* 1 pound ground pork
* 2 eggs
* flavored bread crumbs 1-1.5 cups
* salt pepper red pepper flakes
* .5 tsp Tumeric
* splash lemon juice
* small can tomato sauce

## steps
* Mix all the above
* Roll into balls 
* bake on lightly greased try at 350 for 45-60 min
