## Ingredient
    * 3-4 Kg Cabbage
    * 5 g salt/Kg cabbage
  
    * 1 L water
    * 15 g salt

## Steps
    * Boil salt & water, let cool
    * Shred cabbage
    * Mix salt and cabbage
    * Pack tightly into crock
    * Place stones
    * Cover w/ salt water if required
    * Cover w/ container and fill channel w/ salt water
    * Wait 3-6 weeks
