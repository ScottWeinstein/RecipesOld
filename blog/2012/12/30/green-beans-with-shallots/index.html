Green Beans With Shallots

Dec 30th, 2012 | Comments

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs green beans
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 6 medium shallot
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley
  • 2.5 tsp lemon zest
  • 0.5 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  • Prep
    • wash & trim beans
    • thin slice shallots
    • chop parsley
  • Cook
    • steam or boil beans
    • fry shallots for 5 min
    • add beans, salt, pepper
    • combine parsley, lemon zest and garlic
